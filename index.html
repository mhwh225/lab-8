A quick Fly-by

Left Column - Description

My area is the University of Kentucky. The largest university in Kentucky as well one of the oldest.

The goal of this project is to do a quick fly-by of UKy's campus to show off some of the more notable buildings on a point cloud.

Visualizations created from lidar data provided by KyFromAbove in ArcGIS Pro and Blender.

Page and visualizations created by Miles Whitlock for GEO 409, Department of Geography, University of Kentucky. Spring 2023.
